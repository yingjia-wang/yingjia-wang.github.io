Yingjia WANG (王英嘉)

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), advised by Prof. Ming-Chang Yang.

I am broadly interested in storage systems and have published in top conferences and journals, including HPCA, ICCAD, TC, and TOS. I received my B.Eng degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2022.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  LinkedIn  /  GitHub

Research

I have broad interests in storage systems, including flash storage, computational storage, key-value storage, cloud storage, as well as their interesting intersections. Currently, I am dedicated in innovating storage systems with the emerging data placement technologies such as Zoned Namespace (ZNS) and Flexible Data Placement (FDP).
